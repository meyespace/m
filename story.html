about us

Meyespace is a net art piece created by Erin Mahon, Myrtle Manicad, Fipary Moskalenko and Arwen Phan for NMA867. The game embedded was coded using Unity and the site is hosted on Github. All quotes used in the game experience are sourced from Lucas LaRochelle's Queering the Map net art project.
