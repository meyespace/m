about us

Meyespace is a net art piece created by Erin Mahon, Myrtle Manicad, Fipary Moskalenko and Arwen Phan for NMA867's Technology, Creativity and Identity's Response Media Project. The game embedded was coded using Unity by Arwen Phan and Erin Mahon, and is hosted on this site using Itch.io. This site is hosted on Github.
Illustrations used in the game experience are by Fipary Moskalenko and Myrtle Manicad.

All quotes used in the game experience are sourced from Lucas LaRochelle's Queering the Map net art project, as well as assorted queer texts from the news and internet.

Quotes and images used are sourced from Yulia Mahr's The Church of Our Becoming, RCMP Heritage's The RCMP's Role in the Canadian War on Queers, National Geographic, NationNews' LGBTIs: Treat us as equals, Pogo's Policing Gender: How Surveillance Tech Aids Enforcement of Anti-Trans Laws and The Guardian.
